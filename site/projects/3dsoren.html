Soren, the 3D model

So, 3D art is nowhere near the top of the list of skills I want to learn, and yet here we are. I've been working on that model for far too long already, and I'm still not done!

For now, the modeling is mostly done(don't pay attention to the topology), there's a passable armature with one (1) pose, and some pretty neat shaders.

What I would like to add are some animations, better shaders, and the missing elements (his forearms don't look like what I want, and he needs a panel in the back).

After that, the boy is supposed to be modular, so I have the excuse to make all the extra parts I want!
